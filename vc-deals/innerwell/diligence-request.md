## A. Financials & Unit Economics

**A1. [P0] Current ARR and growth.** Provide TTM ARR as of most recent month-end and a monthly ARR waterfall for the trailing 18 months, broken into: starting ARR, new ARR, expansion ARR, contraction, churn, ending ARR. CSV or Excel preferred.

**A2. [P0] Revenue mix — cash-pay vs in-network insurance.** Quarterly breakdown for the trailing 8 quarters showing absolute dollars and percentage split. Include a brief note on the trajectory of the cash-to-insurance transition.

**A3. [P0] Gross margin by service line.** P&L by service line (psychiatry, therapy, ketamine-assisted psychotherapy) for the trailing 4 quarters showing revenue, direct clinician cost, platform cost, contribution margin, and gross margin %.

**A4. [P0] CAC and LTV by channel.** Customer acquisition cost broken out by channel (paid search, organic / SEO, payer referral, employer / partnership, other), blended CAC, and 12-month LTV by channel and cohort. Provide the underlying CAC calculation methodology.

**A5. [P0] Cash position and runway.** Cash on hand as of most recent month-end, monthly burn (or profit) for the trailing 12 months, and months of runway at current burn. Include WTI debt balance, amortization schedule, and any covenants.

**A6. [P1] Cohort retention.** Patient retention curves at M1, M3, M6, and M12 by acquisition channel, for the most recent 4 quarterly cohorts. CSV preferred.

**A7. [P1] Payback period.** Channel-level CAC payback period in months for ketamine-onboarded patients vs. psychiatry-only and therapy-only patients.
