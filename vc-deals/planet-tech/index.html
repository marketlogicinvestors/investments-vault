3. Thesis — Why This Could Be a Fund-Returner

    >Cattle is the largest underinstrumented protein vertical. ~1.5B head globally; <2% wear connected health devices. Even modest penetration at $10/head/month is a multi-billion-dollar ARR pool. >Vet-channel is a defensible wedge. If vets become the distribution and clinical-trust layer, PlanetTech captures a moat that pure DTC ag-tech (Halter, Vence) does not have. >Hardware → data → carbon optionality. The collar is the trojan horse; the real prize is the longitudinal herd dataset (methane proxies, fertility, disease) — the substrate for carbon credits, insurance, and genetics partnerships. >Pre-seed pricing leaves room. $6M cap is appropriate for unvalidated hardware; if A&M pilot reads out positively, the seed could mark up 3–5x within 12–18 months.
