§ 03

Industry & Market

TAM, structure, competition.

Mercury operates in digital business banking / banking-as-a-platform for startups and SMBs. Primary competitors: Brex, Ramp, and legacy banks filling the post-SVB gap. The 2023 SVB collapse accelerated startup deposit migration toward trusted neobank platforms.
