## One-Sentence Thesis

A decade-old, capital-efficient, founder-led inclusive apparel brand with real product distinction and the strongest repeat rate in its category — projecting a 72× revenue ramp over six years on a $1M round, against ten years of evidence that the business compounds at a steady but sub-venture pace.

---

## Why We're Watching

- 51% repeat purchase rate (2× DTC average) is the single strongest data point in the memo
- $6.7M cumulative lifetime revenue on $1.75M raised = ~3.8× capital efficiency
- Founder has credible media validation: Inc Female Founder 2025, Tory Burch Fellow 2025, CBS Mornings
- Real product differentiation (chest-gap snaps, deep pockets, extended sizing)
- Nordstrom wholesale already live; Wolf & Badger onboarding US + UK
- Underserved category — gender-neutral + size-inclusive is a structural market gap

## Why We're Not Committing

- 10 years to $1.4M revenue — historical growth rate inconsistent with the projection
- "$6.7M run rate" claim does not reconcile against $1.4M 2024 revenue (likely cumulative, not annualized — must be confirmed)
- No tier-1 institutional lead in 10 years of operation is a substantive signal
- 72× growth to $101M by 2030 requires four concurrent strategies (DTC + Retail + Enterprise + Geo) on $1M
- Spain HQ adds tax/operational complexity for US-focused brand
- Cohort retention beyond loyal early adopters is unproven
